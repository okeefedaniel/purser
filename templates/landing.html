What Purser does

Monthly Close Grid

Program × period status at a glance. Submit, review, and approve financial reports in a structured workflow.

Variance Analysis

Automatic variance vs. prior period and vs. budget. Flags outliers before the close package is published.

Compliance Tracking

Document submission deadlines, certifications, and milestones — with automated reminders and escalation.

Harbor Integration

Pulls fiscal activity data directly from Harbor — no manual re-entry of beginning balances or expenditures.

Purser is a DockLabs product built for the Connecticut Department of Economic and Community Development.
